Edinburgh Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Kim Brown | WDD 230 Web Frontend Development | March, 2025

Site Name: Edinburgh Chamber of Commerce

Name was chosen to represent the business community of Edinburgh, Scotland, focusing on networking, economic growth, and advocacy for local businesses.

Site Purpose

Website will serve as a resource for local businesses and the community by:

  • Providing a directory of local businesses and services.
  • Promoting events, networking opportunities, and workshops.
  • Offering membership benefits and advocacy for businesses.
  • Encouraging tourism and investment in Edinburgh.

Home

Welcome to the Edinburgh Chamber of Commerce. Supporting local businesses and economic growth in Edinburgh, Scotland.

Discover

Explore the rich history, demographics, and events that make Edinburgh a great place to do business and visit.

Directory

Find local businesses, organizations, and services that are part of the Edinburgh Chamber of Commerce.

Join

Become a member of the Edinburgh Chamber of Commerce and enjoy networking, advocacy, and promotional opportunities.

Scenarios

  • Business Owner: "What networking events are happening this month?"
  • Investor: "What are the economic growth trends in Edinburgh?"
  • Tourist: "What are some local businesses and attractions I should visit?"

Color Schema

Selected colors for branding and user experience, blue and white scottish flag, green to represent environment sustainblity:

Royal Blue (#royalblue) - Headings 2, Heading 3, and branding.

Green1 (#63EE63E5) - Footer and Header backgrounds.

Green1 (#00f400) - Call-to-action buttons, hoover, highlights.

Dark Grey (#525252) - Headings 1, Text

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings and branding.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text for readability.
