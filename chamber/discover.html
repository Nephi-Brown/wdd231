Facts About Edinburgh

Festivals - Edinburgh's Cultural Pulse

Edinburgh is globally celebrated as the Festival City, home to some of the world’s most iconic cultural events. At the heart of this vibrant scene is the Edinburgh Festival Fringe, the largest arts festival on the planet, which transforms the city every August into a stage for thousands of performers across theatre, comedy, dance, music and more.

Alongside it, the Edinburgh International Festival showcases world-class performances in classical music, opera, and theatre, attracting artists and audiences from across the globe. The Edinburgh International Book Festival welcomes renowned authors and thinkers, while the Royal Edinburgh Military Tattoo, set against the dramatic backdrop of Edinburgh Castle, is a spectacular display of military precision, music and tradition.

These festivals enrich the city’s cultural life, driving tourism, fuelling the local economy. Position Edinburgh as a global hub of creativity and innovation.
